Who is UNI.t?

UNI.T (유니티) is a 9-membered girl group that debuted under KBS on May 18, 2018 with the song 'No More'.
You can follow them on Twitter and Instagram for the latest updates! They also occasionally post on their Filmstagram, where each member posts pictures they've taken of other members or their daily activities.
